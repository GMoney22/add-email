Add an Email Account in cPanel

You can set up a new email account in seconds. Learn how below.


Navigate to Email Accounts

Use cPanel's Email Accounts interface to create an email address. Here's how:

  1. In cPanel, go to Email > Email Accounts.
  2. Click CREATE. A new interface will appear.
  3. Enter your information.

Need help logging in to cPanel? Read our How to Log In documentation.


     

Enter your information

Here you can choose your username and password. Follow these steps:

  1. Enter your username.
  2. Enter a password, or click GENERATE.
  3. Copy your password to a secure location.
  4. Customize any settings.
  5. Click CREATE.
